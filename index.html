School of Engineering

About the School

Explore your passion for learning and innovation with a broad-based curriculum that trains you in core competencies and emerging technologies. Along the way, we will equip you with multidisciplinary skills to develop engineering projects which benefit the community and make your learning more purposeful. You could even be working with professors from NUS, NTU and SUTD on real-world projects in artificial intelligence and data analytics!

Full-Time Courses Offered

Diploma in Aerospace Engineering (N65)

Why AEG?
  • Provide a solid engineering foundation with an emphasis on aerospace Maintainance, Repair, and Overhaul
  • Learn in-demand digital skills in blockchain, robotics, drones, additive manufacturing, data analytics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.
  • Two specialization possibilities are available: Mechanical or Avionics
  • By creating an airborne vehicle, you can gain knowledge of important aerospace principles.

Diploma in Automation & Mechatronic Systems (N50)

Why AMS?
  • A broad-based curriculum with a significant emphasis on mobile, autonomous, and cooperative robotics
  • For intriguing employment chances in robotics engineering and automation, develop your abilities in cutting-edge technologies like augmented reality, robot operating systems, computer vision, and industrial IoT!
  • Select either Autonomous Systems or Automation & Industrial Cybersecurity as your area of expertise.
  • Opportunities to work on projects sponsored by renowned industry partners and cutting-edge start-ups like HOPE Technik, MooVita, Omron Electronics, and Universal Robots

Diploma in Biomedical Engineering (N60)

Why BME?
  • The first and only polytechnic degree that prepares clinical engineering specialists
  • Develop your design, development, and testing abilities for medical devices as well as your manufacturing and cybersecurity skills to take advantage of possibilities in the rapidly expanding medical technology industry.
  • Work on industry projects and participate in selected internships with top MedTech businesses and healthcare organizations including ZOLL Medical and SingHealth.
  • The Polytechnic-SUTD Pathway Programme might help you get a jump start on earning a renowned degree.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering (N43)

Why EE?
  • The only electrical engineering degree in Singapore that focuses specifically on sustainability
  • Strong emphasis on decarbonization, decentralization, and digitalization development sectors so you can take advantage of exciting green energy economic opportunities!
  • Along with elective modules on the green economy, you can select to specialize in either power engineering or clean energy management.
  • Accepted by the Energy Market Authority for the Electrical Technician Licensing Application

Diploma in Electronic & Computer Engineering (N44)

Why ECE?
  • One of Singapore's oldest electronic and computer engineering diploma programs
  • A broad-based curriculum encompassing electronics, including networking, software programming, and semiconductors.
  • Gain in-demand digital skills in data analytics, internet of things (IoT), and artificial intelligence of things (AIoT) to become future-ready!
  • Through industry-sponsored projects and internships with recognized partners like ST Engineering and Micron, get practical knowledge.

Diploma in Engineering Science (N93)

Why ES?
  • This distinctive engineering course, designed for students with a strong interest in Applied Science, places a significant emphasis on mathematics, physics, and computing.
  • Choose between the specializations in Data Analytics & Security or AI for Autonomous Systems to gain in-demand skills for the coming economy.
  • Obtain outstanding scholarships from PSC, A*STAR, and DSTA by gaining important applied R&D experience at regional universities and research centers that will expand your network and competence!

Diploma in Marine & Offshore Technology (N42)

Why MOT?
  • The only diploma that covers both naval architecture and offshore engineering
  • SkillsFuture Work-Study Post-Diploma Programmes to deepen your skills
  • Prestigious ASMI-MOT Scholarship that covers tuition fees and allowances

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (N41)

Why ME?
  • A broad-based education that equips you for a variety of thrilling jobs in fields like precision engineering, transportation, energy and chemicals, engineering services, and more
  • Utilize in-demand digital skills like advanced modeling, industrial automation and robots, and predictive maintenance and analytics to ride the Industry 4.0 wave.
  • With a new sustainability focus, you may assist businesses in maximizing their decarbonization efforts.
  • With industry immersion at businesses like ST Engineering, A*STAR, and TÜV SÜD PSB, there is the chance to specialize in automation design engineering or mobility design engineering.

Common Engineering Programme (N71)

Why CEP?
  • Give yourself more time to identify your interests before choosing one of our eight diplomas.
  • Typical foundational modules introduce you to various engineering specialties
  • A unique induction program gives you early exposure to the sector so you can choose a course wisely.
